# MudForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-13

## Data We Collect

- Account identifiers (character name)
- Authentication data (hashed password)
- Optional email for recovery
- Connection metadata (for security/operations)
- In-game reports and moderation records

## How We Use Data

- Authenticate users
- Operate gameplay and persistence
- Detect abuse and enforce conduct rules
- Troubleshoot reliability and security incidents

## Data Retention

Data is retained while accounts remain active and as needed for moderation/security.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only when required by law or for infrastructure operation.

## Your Controls

- Request account removal through staff
- Request moderation-data review where legally required

## Security

We use technical and operational safeguards, but no system is risk-free.

## Policy Updates

This policy may change over time. Continued use means acceptance of updates.
